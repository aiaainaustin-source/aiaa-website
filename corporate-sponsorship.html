Corporate Sponsorship

for AIAA UT Austin Chapter

Why Become a Sponsor for AIAA UT Austin Chapter

Corporate partners play an essential role in supporting our chapter by connecting students with real-world perspectives from industry. Through sponsorship, companies gain direct access to motivated engineering students while helping create hands-on learning, professional development, and networking opportunities throughout the academic year.

How to Become a Corporate Sponsor

If your organization is interested in partnering with UT Austin AIAA, please reach out to us by email for more information. We are happy to share our corporate sponsorship packet, which outlines available sponsorship levels and partnership opportunities in greater detail.

Sponsorship Levels & Benefits

✦ ✦ ✦ ✦

Bronze Sponsor

  • Support funding for AIAA T-shirts (company recognition on the back of the shirt)
  • Recognition on the UT Austin AIAA website
  • Opportunity to help organize or participate in a social networking event
✦ ✦ ✦ ✦

Silver Sponsor

  • Sponsor an industry-focused current events discussion or
  • Host a company tour for AIAA members
✦ ✦ ✦ ✦

Gold Sponsor

  • Host a general meeting with UT Austin AIAA
  • Opportunity to directly engage with members through presentations, discussions, or Q&A sessions
✦ ✦ ✦ ✦

Platinum Sponsor

  • Includes benefits from all previous sponsorship tiers
  • Additional support toward funding AIAA members' attendance at conferences and professional events
