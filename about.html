About Us

As a professional student organization, AIAA offers many ways for members to get involved. We welcome students from all majors and disciplines to attend our events and discover how AIAA can help them develop their future career interests! No prior experience is required; all you need is curiosity, passion, and a willingness to learn alongside a supportive community!

Networking

AIAA's student events are filled with various industry professionals and faculty from the local Austin area. These events give students an opportunity to get to know more about the growing aerospace industry of Austin while developing major contacts that help students in their future careers.

Volunteerism

At AIAA, we're committed to giving back to the community through meaningful volunteer work. Each semester, our members participate in service projects that support local organizations and make a real impact through hands-on service and outreach.

Competitions

AIAA competes in annual space & aircraft design competitions that give students hands-on engineering experience in a collaborative setting. These teams are especially valuable for underclassmen looking to build technical skills early in their academic journey. Members work together on various projects, applying classroom knowledge to real-world problems.
