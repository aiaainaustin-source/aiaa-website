Who We Are

The UT Austin Chapter of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA) is a professional student organization for people with an interest in aviation and space.

We are dedicated to the advancement of the aerospace engineering profession by providing our members opportunities to develop their professional and social skills.
