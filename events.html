Events

As a professional student organization, AIAA offers multiple events for members to partake in. Examples of events we host are general meetings, workshops, socials, and information sessions! We encourage everyone interested to participate and enjoy the benefits that we give to all UT Austin students!

General Meetings

AIAA's general meetings have representatives ranging from engineers, VPs, and CEOs of various aerospace companies to come to UT Austin to talk about their experiences, the companies they represent, and advice on how to be professional and technical. We also have free food at all of our general meetings, so we encourage students to come over and partake!

Workshops

AIAA's workshops have representatives ranging from advisors, recruiters, and specialists of various aerospace companies to come to UT Austin to give advice to students on how to refine their resumes and cover letters, dress and articulate professionally, and guiding them throughout their career paths!

Socials

AIAA's socials are done in collaboration with various aerospace companies in the greater Austin area, where students have the opportunity to talk to industry professionals about anything career-related or even personal!

Information Sessions

AIAA's information sessions are a way for students to learn more about a specific company that they have an interest in, where they can easily understand their application process, what they want to see in an applicant, and their minimum and preferred requirements for a specific position!
