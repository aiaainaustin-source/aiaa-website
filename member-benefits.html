Member Benefits

AIAA Point System

Members earn points by participating in AIAA activities throughout the semester. These points help recognize active involvement and unlock member benefits. By reaching certain point totals, members become eligible for additional benefits.

How Points Are Earned

  • 5 pts General Meetings
  • 3 pts Social Events
  • 3 pts Volunteering (per half hour)
  • TBA Other Events (announced in advance)

Member Benefit Benchmarks

  • 8 pts Active Member Status
  • 8 pts Resume Bank Access
  • TBA AIAA T-shirts and Stoles
  • TBA Additional benefits

Get Involved

Participation in AIAA events is the best way to make the most of your membership. Whether you're interested in competitions, volunteering, networking, or professional development, there are many ways to get involved and earn points while building valuable experience.
